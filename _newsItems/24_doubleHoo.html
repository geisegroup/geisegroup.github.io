--- date: 2024-12-11 year: 2024 title: Morin & Leroux Pitch Double Hoo Projects text: Graduate students Caroline Morin and Charlie Leroux pitched collaborative undergraduate research projects via the UVA Office of Citizen Scholar Development's Double Hoo program. Their projects, titled Evaluating an Electrolyzer for Selective and Resilient Lithium Hydroxide Production and Developing Selective Membranes for Non-Aqueous Electrochemical Flow Cells, respectively, were selected by the UVA Office of Citizen Scholar Development to be supported during the Summer 2025 term. The Geise Research Group looks forward to welcoming summer students into the group to conduct research on these projects. image: LerouxMorin.jpg ---
