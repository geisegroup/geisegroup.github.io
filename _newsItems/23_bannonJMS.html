--- date: 2023-12-29 year: 2023 title: Bannon Publishes in the Journal of Membrane Science text: Graduate research assistant Sean Bannon published Influence of Donnan and dielectric exclusion on ion sorption in sulfonated polysulfones has been accepted for publication in the Journal of Membrane Science.
