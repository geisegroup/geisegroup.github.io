--- date: 2021-9-23 year: 2021 title: McCormack Publishes Thermodynamic Description of Cross-Over in Non-Aqueous Redox Flow Battery Membranes text: Graduate research assistant Patrick McCormack (along with co-advisor Prof. Gary Koenig) published Thermodynamic interactions as a descriptor of cross-over in nonaqueous redox flow battery membranes in ACS Applied Materials & Interfaces. This paper reports the use of regular solution theory to describe thermodynamic interactions that influence membrane cross-over of organic redox active molecules in non-aqueous redox flow batteries. image: pm.jpg ---
