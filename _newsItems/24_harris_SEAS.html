--- date: 2024-4-24 year: 2024 title: Anna Harris wins 2024 Outstanding Student Award text: Fourth year undergraduate researcher Anna Harris won the Class of 2024 UVA Engineering Outstanding Student Award, which recognizes fourth year undergraduate students who have excelled in the areas of academics, leadership, and service. Anna, the second Geise Research Group undergraduate researcher to win the award, has been a member of the team for three years now, and she will be missed next year as she begins her career in pharmaceutical research and development.
