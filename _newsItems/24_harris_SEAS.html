--- date: 2024-4-24 year: 2024 title: Anna Harris wins SEAS Outstanding Student Award text: Fourth year undergraduate researcher Anna Harris won the Class of 2024 SEAS Outstanding Student Award. This award, which is presented by the UVA School of Engineering and Applied Science (SEAS), recognizes fourth year undergraduate students who have excelled in the areas of academics, leadership, and service. Anna, the second Geise Research Group undergraduate researcher to win the award, has been an active undergraduate researcher in the group for three years now, and she will be missed next year as she begins her career in pharmaceutical research and development. Congratulations Anna! image: harris.jpg ---
